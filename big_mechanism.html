Big Mechanism

Automated Discovery of Cancer Pathway Models from Biomedical Text

ABOUT

The goal of this project is to revolutionize cancer biology (and discovery science in general) by developing capabilities that will allow to -

  1. Automatically read biomedical literature on cancer pathways
  2. Extract causal fragments from the literature
  3. Assemble those fragments into a full causal model of a cancer pathway.

BM is a highly interdisciplinary project that involves ideas and people from various disciplines – cancer & system biology, machine learning, natural language process
